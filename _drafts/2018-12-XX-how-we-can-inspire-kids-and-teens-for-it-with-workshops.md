# Introduction
One of the best things you can do in your job career is to share your experiences and knowledge with other people.
Whether you introduce a new colleague to his future tasks, being a mentor or trainer, writing articles and giving talks about work related topics or leading a whole workshop for your colleagues - there are numerous possibilities to both educate and fascinate other people.

A very special way to do this is inspiring kids and teens to follow in your footsteps.
This is what I recently do a lot in my free time by attending workshops for kids as a mentor.
In this article I want to write about my own experiences and why you absolutely should do the same thing right now :)!
